Spectra Scientific Inc

Spectra Scientific is a rental and engineering company dedicated to innovation withing the groundwater sampling industry. Founded in 1999 with the goal of creating quality products that raise the industry standard and contribute a positive legacy. Our Rental division was founded on the principles of educating and simplifying the work of clients by providing quality products and world class support.
