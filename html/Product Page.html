RKI GX-6000

Air Quality

Description

The RKI GX-6000 is a power Lithium battery powered 6 gas meter measuring CH4, O2, H2S, CO, VOC, and HCN.  It is powered by a Lithium battery capable of lasting 14 hours on a single charge. 

The RKI GX-6000 multi gas feature makes the industry standard for VOC sampling in enclosed areas.
